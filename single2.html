My Reflection for this Project

Lorem ipsum dolor amet nullam consequat etiam feugiat

When I first started this project it's made me thing that making a webpage that is dedicated for the activities that we've created since the start of the semester is quite hard and tiresome, considering that I don't have much any background when it comes to webpage design, but as I have learned and familiarized myself on the composition of the webpage template that I have chosen, it somehow became easy as there's not really much work to procure plus it's quite fun to remodel a webpage you're interested in into your own. As seen on the finished product, the webpage is composed of buttons that will redirect into the repository of each respective Hands-on activity. Additionally, there's also a brief introduction to myself, and of course this reflection itself, the things that I have learned from this course, and lastly is the link in which the user will be redirected into the course itself. Overall, this project can be tedious but extremely enjoyable to make, considering the fact that I have widen up my knowledge towards webpage designing even a little bit.
